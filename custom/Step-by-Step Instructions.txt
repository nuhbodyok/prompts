**Prompt Template for Generating Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Task Definition:**
   - Clearly articulate the specific task or process you need instructions for.
   - Indicate your goal with precision.

2. **Coverage and Limitations:**
   - Outline what should be included in the instructions and what should be omitted.
   - State any limitations, such as time frames, resources, or specific conditions.

3. **Requirements:**
   - Specify any prior knowledge, skills, or materials necessary before commencing the task.

4. **Expected Result:**
   - Illustrate what successful completion of the task entails.

5. **Progress Indicators:**
   - Identify any checkpoints or signs that signal advancement throughout the process.

**Example Prompt for AI Chatbot:**

"Create step-by-step instructions for preparing a cup of coffee with a French press.

1. **Coverage and Limitations:**
   - Include details on grinding coffee beans, heating water, and the brewing process.
   - Exclude any instructions related to milk preparation or other brewing techniques.
   - Keep the entire process within a 10-minute timeframe, using standard kitchen tools.

2. **Requirements:**
   - Tools: French press, coffee grinder, kettle, measuring spoon, mug.
   - Ingredients: Whole coffee beans, water.

3. **Expected Result:**
   - A freshly brewed cup of coffee that balances flavor well.

4. **Progress Indicators:**
   - Verify that the coffee bean grind is coarse.
   - Check that the water reaches the proper temperature before starting the brew.

Please provide instructions based on the above details."
